I am deeply in love with mother nature and natural lighting and I am constantly blown away by the beauty of our planet. Growing up in Arizona has been such an amazing experience. It is a harsh environment and one of the most beautiful places in the world. I have been photographing since 2006 and have learned so much along the way. I am so thankful for my clients and that I am able to express myself through photos and make a living from it.

In my spare time you can find me reading in my hammock, backpacking with my love in the mountains, singing to my garden, playing with my son or howling at the moon with my soul sisters in the forest.

Thanks for stopping by! -Linds
