A vintage style skate cruiser handcrafted and hand-designed.

Handcrafted and designed cruiser boards built by a true skater. Learning woodworking with my dad at a young age in between skateboarding, I set out to create my twin son's first board. After finishing the boards, and so many people asking for one, I felt the best thing I can do is help support the sport that has defined my life.

All original handcrafted oak wood design. Order your handcrafted board and choose from red, blue, green or black wheels with matching colored riser pads. Boards run $189.99 complete +shipping. Rush orders can be requested for an additional fee.
